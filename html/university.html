In order to register for the ELPT+M exams at AUCA, the following documents must be submitted to the admission committee no later than three working days before the officially scheduled dates of the entrance exams:

Participant registration form (available from the Admissions Office, room G20) Original and photocopy of passport (or birth certificate and ID if the applicant does not already have a passport) 1 color photograph 3 x 4 cm (white background) Receipt confirming the payment of 1000 soms (or 15 US dollars) for passing the entrance exam to AUCA
